Frequently Asked Questions

Is Dfamo really free?

Yes! Dfamo is currently free to use for all students during our Alpha phase. We believe education should be accessible.

Do you store my essays?

No. We value privacy. We do not store your prompts or generated essays. Everything happens in your browser session.

Is the content plagiarism-free?

Dfamo generates unique content based on your prompt using AI. However, we always recommend checking the output with a plagiarism checker and adding your own citations.

Does it work on mobile?

Absolutely. Dfamo is fully responsive and optimized for smartphones and tablets.

What languages are supported?

Currently, we support English (US/UK), Spanish, and French. Select your preferred language in the input settings.
